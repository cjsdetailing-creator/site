About Me

Hello! My name is Cav, and I’m the founder of CJ’s Detailing Shop. I’ve built a strong passion for automotive care and take pride in delivering high-quality detailing services that leave every vehicle looking its absolute best. Although I’m 14, I bring hands-on experience working with a wide variety of vehicles and detailing needs. I understand that age might raise questions, but my focus has always been on skill, attention to detail, and consistent results. I’m fully equipped to handle everything from basic interior and exterior details to more advanced services such as paint correction and ceramic coatings. I’m based in Turlock, and I’m committed to providing reliable, professional-level service with every job. My goal is simple: to treat every car as if it were my own and ensure every customer drives away satisfied with the results.
